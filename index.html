OUR FOOD PRODUCTS

ALFALFA (LUCERNE)

Alfalfa is actually neither hay nor grass! It is in fact a legume and is also grown as a richer type of animal feed. Alfalfa hay has a much higher protein level than grass / hay, which makes it very fattening to feed as a main ingredient for an average adult rabbit.

 Note: Alfalfa hay also has a significantly higher calcium content (1.5% compared to 0.5% in grass hay), so rabbits with a history of calcium-related issues, such as bladder sludge, should avoid it.
